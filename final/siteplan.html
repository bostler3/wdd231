Website Planning Document

Site Name: Pro Soccer Fan Zone

This name represents a site that provides information for pro soccer fans, focusing on a few popular leagues.

Site Purpose

This site will show the teams in each league, a page with team rosters and player data, and a page that allows a user to (pretend) purchase tickets to games.

Scenarios

Questions a site user might ask:

  • Which teams are in the English Premier League?
  • Who plays for FC Barcelona?
  • When is LAFC's next game and how do I purchase tickets to it?

Colors

Color ideas:

  • Header/Footer: background color is #393F86 and font color is white
  • Main: background color is white and font color is black
  • Other colors: where appropriate and color-contrast compliant, try to incorporate the English Premier League's purple color (#3D195B); La Liga's red color (#FF4B44); and MLS' blue (#001F5B) and red (#E2231A)
